About Me

Hi, my name is Rachel. It's nice to meet you. I am currently a stay home mother of four children. I previously worked in the veterinary field as a veterinary assistant. I did everything from assisting the veterinarians with preventive care appointments to assisting in complex surgeries. Due to lifes twists and turns I was unable to pursue my passion with animals in becoming a licensed veterinary technician. One day out of nowhere I decided to spontaneously make a transition into web development and discovered a new passion. In 2021 I enrolled in a local coding bootcamp for Full Stack Web Development and completed it in June of 2022.

While in the process of job hunting I knew I wanted to learn as much as I could. At that point I decided to go back to college and I am currently enrolled and in the process of attaining an associates degree in Web and Mobile Development. My projected graduation is summer of 2024.

My current goal is to help small business owners, such as stay at home crafters, to build custom websites to attract customers to purchase their products. This will greatly help me to gain more experience working with clients and buildig better websites. Have an idea? Go on over to my contact me page and lets get the ball rolling!
